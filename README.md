# Interactive Bilingual Furniture Store

Open `index.html` in a browser.

## What is included
- Fully bilingual EN/FR interface.
- Product categories for Outdoor, Living, Dining, Bedroom, Bath, Lighting, Decor, Art & Mirrors, Rugs, Office, Sale, and Trade.
- Unique loaded product imagery for every category.
- Product detail pages for every product with gallery images, options, quantity selector, saved list, and add-to-bag drawer.
- Category hover slideshows on the homepage.
- Scroll reveal animations on the main page.

Keep `index.html`, `styles.css`, `script.js`, and the `assets` folder together.
